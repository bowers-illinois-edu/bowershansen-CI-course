# Actual topic sequence, as taught (basis for the 2027 schedule)

This records what the 2026 course actually covered, day by day, so the 2027
schedule reflects the real sequence rather than the older plan. Dates below are
2027 placeholders (mirror of the 2026 structure); confirm against the official
ICPSR 2027 calendar. In 2027 Juneteenth (June 19) falls on a Saturday, so there
is no weekday holiday inside the course.

Section 1: Randomized experiments (application: Arceneaux 2005, ACORN GOTV)
- Day 1 (Mon): Tom; introduction, random allocation, potential outcomes, Fisher's exact test
- Day 2 (Tue): Tom; the sharp (Fisherian) framework: testing sharp nulls, constant-effect models, confidence sets
- Day 3 (Wed): Tom; the weak (Neyman) framework I: average effects, the difference-in-means, unbiasedness, consistency
- Day 4 (Thu): Tom; the weak framework II: conservative variance, normal approximation, testing, confidence intervals
- (Fri: no class; HW 1 due)

Section 2: Adjustment and imperfect experiments (application: Albertson and Lawrence 2009)
- Day 5 (Mon): Tom; covariance adjustment: blocking, gain scores, regression adjustment of experiments
- Day 6 (Tue): Tom; noncompliance and attrition: the CACE, instrumental variables, bounds

Section 3: Observational studies and matching (application: Gilligan and Sergenti 2008, UN peacekeeping)
- Day 7 (Wed): Tom; observational studies and building a matched design: distances, forming matches
- Day 8 (Thu): Tom; evaluating a matched design: balance and effective sample size
- Day 9 (Fri): Tom; inference under as-if randomization: the sharp and weak frameworks (HW 2 due)
- Day 10 (Mon): Jake; sensitivity analysis: hidden bias in a matched design (Gamma, amplification, regression-based bounds)
- Day 11 (Tue): Jake; why "controlling for" is not a research design: specification curves, extrapolation, influential points

Section 4: Beyond the binary-treatment matched pair (application: V-Dem, democratization and corruption)
- Day 12 (Wed): Jake; non-bipartite matching: continuous and multivalued treatments, dose-response
- Day 13 (Thu): Jake; longitudinal designs: risk-set matching versus difference-in-differences; parallel trends and HonestDiD (HW 3 due)
